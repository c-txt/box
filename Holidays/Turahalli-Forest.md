# Turahalli Forest

The Last Surviving Forest Within Bangalore City Limits
Located just off Kanakapura Road, this deciduous forest becomes a dreamscape during rains. No entry fees. No curated signs. Just rock, tree, and time. [[Avalahalli Forest|Avalahalli Forest]]

Early morning visits offer a soundtrack of peacocks and parakeets.
Trek to the hilltop Shiva temple as mist rolls off granite boulders.
Bring a flask. There are no shops here. Just solitude. In a city that's always talking, Turahalli listens.
