# Crunchy Ragi And Oats Breakfast Bowl
#recipe

Ingredients (1 serving)

- 1 tablespoon Ragi Flour (Finger Millet/ Nagli)

- 2 tablespoons Instant Oats (Oatmeal)

- 1/8 cup Water

- 1/2 cup Milk

- Honey , to taste

- 4 Multigrain Digestive Ragi Biscuits , broken

- 1 tablespoon Badam (Almond) , sliced

- 1 tablespoon Pistachios , sliced

How to make Crunchy Ragi And Oats Breakfast Bowl Recipe 

1.  To begin making the Crunchy Ragi & Oats Breakfast Bowl Recipe,
    firstly take a saucepan and switch on the heat.

2.  Into the pan, add 1 tablespoon Ragi Flour, 2 tablespoons Instant
    Oats, 1/8 cup Water, 1/2 cup Milk, Honey to taste and mix it well.

3.  Cook the mixture till it becomes thick - this will take about a
    couple of minutes.

4.  Once done, switch off the heat and allow the Ragi oats porridge to
    cool. 

5.  Once the Ragi & Oats Porridge cool down, we will now assemble the
    porridge to make a breakfast jar.

6.  Into a small jar, add the broken NutriChoice Diabetic Friendly
    Essentials Ragi Cookies to the bottom of the har.

7.  Spoon the Ragi & Oats Porridge over the cookies.

8.  Add sliced bananas and sprinkle some chopped almonds and pistachios
    on top. It is ready to be served. 

9.  Serve Crunchy Ragi & Oats Breakfast Bowl for your breakfast on its
    own or along with a Fried Eggs and Toast for a wholesome breakfast.
