# How to cook chicken in the Airfryer

#recipe

## **Ingredients**

**Marinated Boneless chicken breast**: substitute with boneless chicken thighs or chicken drumsticks

## **How to cook marinated chicken in the air fryer**

**Prep the chicken:remove any unwanted fat from the chicken breast and transfer it to a Ziploc bag or a large bowl.

**Make the marinade:** In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic granules, Herbs de Provence, salt, and black pepper until combined

**Marinate the chicken:** pour the chicken marinade over the chicken breast and massage it gently with your hand until the chicken is well coated. Let it sit and marinate for at least 30 minutes or up to 12 hours in the fridge.

**Air fry the chicken:** Preheat the air fryer at 390F/195C for 3-5 minutes then spray the air fryer basket with cooking oil. Place the chicken in a single layer in the air fryer basket and cook for a total of 18 to 20 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165F/74C flipping it halfway through.

Remove the chicken from the air fryer basket to a plate, cover loosely with foil and leave to rest for about 5 minutes before cutting into it. Serve immediately with your favorite sides. Enjoy!

## **How to store and reheat**

Cooked chicken can be stored in the fridge for up to 4 days in an airtight container or in the freezer for up to 2 months.

Reheat the chicken in the air fryer, oven, or microwave until warmed through to your liking.
