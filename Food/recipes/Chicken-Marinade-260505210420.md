#### Components of a Marinade:

* **Acid**: Common acids used include vinegar, lemon juice, wine, and yoghurt. Acid breaks down proteins in meat, which can make it more tender.
* **Oil**: Oils like olive oil or vegetable oil help distribute the marinade’s flavours evenly and keep the food moist.
* **Flavourings**: Herbs, spices, garlic, onions, soy sauce, mustard, and other flavourings are added to impart specific tastes to the food.

### Ingredients for Basic Indian Marinade

To prepare this simple Indian chicken marinade, you will need the following ingredients:

* 1 cup plain yogurt
* 2 tablespoons lemon juice
* 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
* 1 teaspoon ground cumin
* 1 teaspoon ground coriander
* 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
* 1 teaspoon paprika
* 1 teaspoon garam masala
* 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
* Salt to taste
